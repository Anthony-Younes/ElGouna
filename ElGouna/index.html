Welcome to EL Gouna!

A State Of Mind

A TOWN TO LOVE Welcome to El Gouna,
an enchanting destination that invites you to discover and connect with the awe-inspiring Red Sea.
This exclusive, laid-back town promises convenient seaside living in an exclusive, self-contained community.
Just 25km from Hurghada, El Gouna is a four-hour flight from Europe's major capitals.

Anthony Younes' Personal Review of El Gouna Greetings! I'm Anthony Younes, a resident of El Gouna for a total of seven wonderful years. Currently, I'm a student at EGIS (El Gouna International School), and I couldn't be more thrilled to share my experiences living in this extraordinary village. El Gouna, to me, isn't just a place; it's a vibrant tapestry of experiences and memories woven into the fabric of my life. When you step into El Gouna, you're instantly transported to a world that defies the norms of Egypt. It's not merely a tourist destination; it's a haven where cultures converge, and time seems to slow down amidst the azure waters and picturesque landscapes. Despite being a hotspot for events and holidays, El Gouna retains its essence of tranquility and serenity for us residents. Yes, the streets might buzz with activity during peak seasons, but there's always a quiet corner or a secluded spot where you can retreat and revel in the peaceful ambiance. It's this delicate balance between vibrancy and tranquility that sets El Gouna apart. Living here, you're never alone, yet you're never overwhelmed by crowds. There's a comforting sense of community that envelops you, ensuring that while you have the company of fellow residents, you also have the space to savor moments of solitude and introspection. In essence, El Gouna isn't just a village; it's a sanctuary where the rhythm of life aligns perfectly with the ebb and flow of the Red Sea. Whether you're strolling along the waterfront promenade, exploring the vibrant markets, or simply basking in the warmth of the sun-kissed shores, every moment in El Gouna feels like a cherished memory in the making. If you're seeking a destination that seamlessly blends the allure of a tourist hotspot with the tranquility of a hidden gem, look no further than El Gouna. Here, amidst the azure waters and swaying palms, you'll discover a slice of paradise that feels like home from the moment you arrive.
